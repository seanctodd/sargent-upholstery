## Frequently Asked Questions

<div class="faq-item">

### Do I need an appointment?

No appointment is needed — walk-ins are welcome during business hours. Feel free to stop by anytime Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM – 4:30 PM. You can also call us at [904.355.2529](tel:+19043552529) or email [sales@sargentupholstery.com](mailto:sales@sargentupholstery.com) if you'd like to schedule a specific time.

</div>

<div class="faq-item">

### Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We offer free, no-obligation estimates. Bring your vehicle by during business hours and we'll assess the work needed and provide a detailed quote.

</div>

<div class="faq-item">

### What types of vehicles do you work on?

We work on all types of vehicles — from classic cars and daily drivers to commercial trucks and fleet vehicles. Whether it's a vintage restoration or a modern upgrade, we have the expertise to handle it.

</div>

<div class="faq-item">

### How long does a typical job take?

Turnaround time varies depending on the scope of work. Simple repairs may be completed in a day, while full interior restorations can take several weeks. We'll provide a time estimate along with your quote.

</div>

<div class="faq-item">

### Do you offer a shuttle service?

Yes, we offer a complimentary shuttle service in the downtown Jacksonville area so you can drop off your vehicle without any hassle.

</div>

<div class="faq-item">

### What materials do you work with?

We work with a wide range of materials including leather, vinyl, canvas, carpet, and specialty fabrics. We'll help you select the right material for your project.

</div>
